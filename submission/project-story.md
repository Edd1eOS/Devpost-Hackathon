# Project Story — Next Audit

## Inspiration
We identified a major inefficiency in traditional auditing workflows: most auditors rely on sample-based reviews due to time and resource constraints, meaning large portions of expense data are never checked.

This creates an opportunity for AI-assisted systems to perform scalable first-pass auditing across entire datasets rather than limited samples.

---

## The Idea
Next Audit is an AI Auditor Assistant designed to automate business expense verification by combining OCR extraction, AI-assisted receipt understanding, and deterministic audit rules.

Businesses and auditors can upload complete expense records and receipts, allowing the system to review all expenses and flag suspicious, invalid, or inconsistent entries.

---

## Why This Matters
Current auditing processes are:
- Time intensive
- Labour expensive
- Limited in scale
- Prone to missed duplicate or suspicious claims

Next Audit aims to reduce human effort while increasing audit coverage and consistency.

---

## Vision
Our long-term vision is a scalable auditing platform where companies can configure their own audit rules and policies, enabling tailored AI-assisted compliance workflows for different businesses.

---

## What We Learned
Through this project, we explored:
- OCR technology for structured receipt extraction
- Hybrid AI + deterministic logic architectures
- Spec-driven development workflows
- Designing configurable rule engines for scalable auditing systems

---

## Challenges
The main challenge was balancing AI flexibility with the reliability requirements of auditing.

To address this, we adopted a hybrid model where AI handles extraction and anomaly assistance, while deterministic rule-based logic makes final audit decisions.
